Content rules for every post
🎯
One hook per post
Every piece of content leads with a single visual or copy hook. Don't try to communicate the product AND the deal AND the brand in the first second. Pick one and build around it.
📅
Deal urgency is always "today only"
Every caption should reinforce that Terrible Tuesday is a one-day deal. "Today only," "This Tuesday," and "Stop in today" are the FOMO triggers that drive same-day foot traffic.
📱
First 1–2 seconds are the whole ad
On TikTok and Reels, most viewers bail before the third second. The opening shot is non-negotiable — it either hooks or loses. Every shot list starts with the strongest visual.
🏷️
Price goes in the caption, not the concept
Deal prices aren't finalized at the time of shooting. Keep price out of on-screen graphics so videos can be reused across multiple Tuesdays or updated without a reshoot.
🤙
Terrible's Own = brand equity, not just deals
Car Wash, Fountain Drink, and Medium Coffee aren't just Tuesday promos — they're owned experiences. Treat them as brand moments, not just price posts.
🔗
Always include "link in bio" for multi-deal weeks
When multiple products are on deal the same Tuesday, use "link in bio for the full lineup" in every individual product caption. This drives Terrible's+ app engagement and repeat visits.
